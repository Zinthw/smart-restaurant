# Communication Plan - Smart Restaurant

## 1. Communication Channels

| Channel | Purpose | Participants | Frequency |
|---------|---------|--------------|-----------|
| **Discord/Slack** | Daily communication | All team | Continuous |
| **GitHub Issues** | Bug tracking, tasks | All team | As needed |
| **GitHub PRs** | Code review | All team | Per feature |
| **Video Call** | Meetings, demos | All team | Weekly |
| **Shared Drive** | Documents, assets | All team | As needed |

---

## 2. Meeting Schedule

### Daily Standup
- **Time:** 9:00 AM
- **Duration:** 15 minutes max
- **Format:**
  - What I did yesterday
  - What I'll do today
  - Any blockers?

### Sprint Review (Every 4 days)
- **Duration:** 30 minutes
- **Agenda:**
  1. Demo completed features
  2. Review sprint goals
  3. Plan next sprint

### Sprint Retrospective (End of Sprint)
- **Duration:** 20 minutes
- **Agenda:**
  1. What went well?
  2. What could improve?
  3. Action items
